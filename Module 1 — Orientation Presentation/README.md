### Topic: The AI Agent Factory

#### Simple Summary

AI Agent Factory ka main idea yeh hai ke AI sirf chat karne ke liye nahi, balkay **real kaam karne wale digital workers** ban sakte hain. In digital workers ko **AI Worker** ya **Digital FTE** kaha jata hai.

#### Key Points

* **AI Worker / Digital FTE** = AI jo real tasks perform karta hai.
* **General Agent** = ChatGPT, Claude, OpenCode jese assistants.
* **AI-Native Company** = Choti human team + bohat se AI Workers.
* AI Worker sirf model nahi, balkay **complete system** hota hai.
* Reliable AI Worker ke 4 parts:

  1. Structured Specifications
  2. Domain Expertise
  3. Engineering Architecture
  4. Human Oversight
* AI ka goal human ko replace karna nahi, balkay uski productivity barhana hai.
* AI Workers repetitive aur structured knowledge work ko automate karte hain.
* Human final approval aur supervision rakhta hai.

#### Important Terms

* **Digital FTE** = AI Employee
* **AI Worker** = Digital Worker
* **AI-Native Company** = Company jo AI Workers ke sath operate kare
* **Human Oversight** = Human monitoring aur approval
