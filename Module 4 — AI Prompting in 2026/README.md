### Topic: AI Models (Part 2)

#### Simple Summary

AI **Model** AI ka brain hota hai. Har model ki apni **strengths aur weaknesses** hoti hain. Har task ke liye best model alag ho sakta hai.

#### Key Points

* **Model** = AI ka trained brain.
* Ek hi company ke multiple models ho sakte hain.
* Har model har kaam mein best nahi hota.
* Kuch models coding mein strong hote hain, kuch writing ya reasoning mein.
* Zaroorat ke hisaab se model choose karo.

#### Common Model Types

* **General Model** = Har type ke tasks.
* **Reasoning Model** = Complex problems solve karne ke liye.
* **Coding Model** = Programming aur debugging.
* **Multimodal Model** = Text + Image + Audio samajhta hai.

#### Important Terms

* **Model** = Trained AI system.
* **Parameters** = Model ka learned knowledge.
* **Multimodal** = Multiple input types handle karna.
* **Reasoning Model** = Deep thinking ke liye optimized model.

**Main Idea:** Har AI model ki capability different hoti hai. **Right task ke liye right model** choose karna best results deta hai.
